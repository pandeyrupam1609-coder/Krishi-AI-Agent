# ✨ Features

## 🌱 Crop Identification

- Identify crop from uploaded image
- Detect crop variety
- Estimate growth stage

---

## 🦠 Disease & Pest Detection

- Detect diseases
- Detect pests
- Detect nutrient deficiencies
- Highlight infected regions
- Confidence score
- Explain causes

---

## 🌦 Live Weather

- Current weather
- 7-Day Forecast
- Rain prediction
- Heatwave alerts
- Frost alerts
- Wind alerts
- Weather-based recommendations

---

## 💊 Smart Medicine Recommendation

- Fungicides
- Insecticides
- Pesticides
- Dosage
- Spray method
- Waiting period
- Safety precautions

---

## 🌿 Fertilizer Recommendation

Recommendations based on

- Crop
- Growth Stage
- Disease
- Nutrient Deficiency

Includes

- NPK
- Organic alternatives
- Application timing
- Dosage

---

## 💧 Irrigation Planner

- Smart irrigation schedule
- Rain-aware recommendations
- Soil moisture support
- Overwatering alerts

---

## 📈 Market Intelligence

- Nearby Mandi Prices
- Best Selling Market
- Price Comparison
- Selling Recommendation

---

## 🏛 Government Schemes

Suggests

- PM-KISAN
- PMFBY
- KCC
- State Subsidies
- Agriculture Loans
- Farmer Welfare Programs

# 🔒 Safety

- Sustainable farming first
- Recommend chemicals only when necessary
- Include dosage instructions
- Include PPE guidance
- Include waiting period before harvest
- Encourage consultation with local agricultural officers for uncertain diagnoses
